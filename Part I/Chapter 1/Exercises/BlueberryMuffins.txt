Baking Blueberry Muffins (with Steps and Glossary):

Ingredients:

2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup butter
1 egg
1 cup blueberries
2 teaspoons baking powder

Steps:

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
Grease a muffin pan (12 cups) or line it with paper muffin cups.
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and baking powder.
Melt the butter in a microwave for 30 seconds, then add to the dry ingredients.
Add milk and egg, and mix the batter until just combined.
Fold in the blueberries gently with a spoon.
Pour the batter evenly into the muffin pan.
Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 20–25 minutes.
Remove the pan from the oven using oven mitts.
Let the muffins cool for 5 minutes before removing them from the pan.

Glossary:

Muffin pan: A baking tray with 12 round compartments.
Preheat: Heat the oven to the specified temperature before baking.
Fold: Gently combine ingredients without stirring vigorously.
Oven mitts: Insulated gloves used to protect hands when handling hot pans.
