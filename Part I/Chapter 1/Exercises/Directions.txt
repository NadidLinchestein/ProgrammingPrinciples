Directions from Home to a Classroom:

Think of the layout of your neighborhood, school, and transportation, then break it down into clear steps.

From the front door of your apartment to a classroom:

Step out of your apartment.
Walk straight to the elevator and press the "down" button.
Enter the elevator and press "1" for the ground floor.
Exit the elevator and walk outside.
Turn right and walk to the bus stop.
Board bus number 12.
Ride for 10 minutes and get off at the "Swinburne University" stop.
Walk straight ahead until you reach the main entrance of the school.
Enter through the front doors, then turn left.
Walk down the hall to room 101.
