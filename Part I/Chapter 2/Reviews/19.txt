Q)  What operators can you apply to an int?


A)  You can apply various operators to an integer, including:

    Arithmetic operators: +, -, *, /, %
    Assignment operators: =, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=
    Increment/decrement operators: ++, --
    Comparison operators: ==, !=, <, >, <=, >=
    Logical operators: &&, ||, !
    Bitwise operators: &, |, ^, ~, <<, >>
